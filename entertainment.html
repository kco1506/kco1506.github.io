Entertainment
Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are plenty of other things to do!

  • Visit the local history museum and art galleries
  • Chartered fishing tours
  • Rainforest zip-lining and snorkeling
  • Visit the many pubs, including a microbrewery
  • Visit the new dance club or catch a movie
  • Helicopter rides
  • Arcade and bowling
  • Coming Soon: Nine hole golf course
Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
